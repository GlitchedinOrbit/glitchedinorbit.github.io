Here you can find an overview of planets, moons, space stations, and surface locations, plus terrain and weather, and other basic information including ship and ground vehicle spawning in Star Citizen — updated as of the 3.13.1 patch.

In the table below, the ☉︎ symbol indicates a solar system, the ◯︎ symbol a planet, the ☾︎ symbol a moon, and the 🛰︎ symbol a space station. The ⤷︎ symbol means that the space station next to it belongs to the planet or moon listed above it. The locations in bold are considered major locations. Furthermore, the hexagon ⬡︎ column indicates from which orbital marker (OM) a surface location is visible; it specifically uses the solid hexagon seen in-game, rather than the dashed hexagon.
